Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground meat (pork or lean beef)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 can tomato suace
  • 2 tablespoons of parsley
  • 1 crush clove of garlic
  • 1 dash of sugar
  • seasonings: dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • 1 pk. lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, Parmesan, and shredded mozzarella for layers
  • Eggs

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook the meat until brown and crumbly. Add onion and tomato sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the chesse layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Make the lasagna
