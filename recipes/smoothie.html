Avocado Smoothie

Smooth and sweet avocado smoothie with fill you up and chill you out

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 rip avocado, halved and pitted
  • 1/2 cup of vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons of honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Cooking instructions

  1. Place milk, avocado, and honey into blender
  2. Add ice cubes
  3. blend until smooth
